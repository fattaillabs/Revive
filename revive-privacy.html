Privacy Policy

Re:vive — Last updated: February 20, 2026

Our commitment is simple: your conversations stay on your device. Re:vive is built with a privacy-first architecture. All AI processing happens entirely on-device using Apple Intelligence. We never see, collect, or store your chat data on our servers.

1. Data That Stays on Your Device

Re:vive processes all data locally on your device. The following data is created and stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Imported chat histories — WhatsApp conversations you import are parsed and stored locally.
  • AI-generated conversations — All responses are generated on-device using Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models). No data is sent to any cloud AI service.
  • Conversation memory and embeddings — Vector databases used for memory recall are created and stored locally.
  • Style profiles and relationship data — Analysis of conversation patterns remains on your device.
  • User preferences and settings — Your name, preferences, and app settings are stored locally using standard iOS storage.

2. iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, Re:vive may back up your conversation data to your personal iCloud account. This is handled entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure.

  • iCloud backups are encrypted and managed by Apple.
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data.
  • You can disable iCloud backup for Re:vive in your device's Settings.

For more information on how Apple handles iCloud data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Third-Party Advertising

Re:vive displays ads to free-tier users through Google AdMob. In connection with serving ads, the following may apply:

  • Google AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and general usage data for ad personalization and analytics.
  • Ad data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • You can limit ad tracking through your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Premium subscribers do not see ads, and no ad-related data collection occurs for premium users.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

To be clear, we do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your imported WhatsApp conversations
  • Messages you send or receive within Re:vive
  • Names, phone numbers, or contact information
  • Photos, media, or attachments
  • Your location data
  • Any personal information beyond what is described above

5. On-Device AI Processing

Re:vive uses Apple Intelligence (on-device Foundation Models) to generate AI responses. This means:

  • All AI processing happens on your device — nothing is sent to external servers.
  • No conversation data leaves your device for AI processing.
  • Apple's on-device models run without an internet connection.

6. Children's Privacy

Re:vive is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of Re:vive after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
